1. Introduction

Welcome to Mitara.AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Mitara.AI is an AI-powered nutrition and calorie tracking application that helps users achieve their health and fitness goals through intelligent food recognition, personalized meal planning, and evidence-based educational content.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our App.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted in our database using AES-256 encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text
  • Row-Level Security: Database access controls ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth 2.0 implementation for Google and Apple Sign-In

5.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting employee access to user data
  • Incident response procedures for potential security breaches
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices
Important: While we use commercially reasonable efforts to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

6.1 Active Accounts

  • Account data, health data, meal logs: Retained while your account is active
  • Food photos: Retained in your account until you delete them or delete your account
  • Voice recordings: Transcribed to text and immediately deleted (not stored)

6.2 Deleted Accounts

  • When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days of account deletion
  • Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for research and analytics

6.3 Legal Retention

We may retain certain information for longer periods if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, dispute resolution).

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • View Your Data: Access all your data within the App (Settings → Account → My Data)
  • Export Your Data: Request a copy of your data in a portable format (contact us at privacy@mitara.ai)

7.2 Correction and Update

  • Update Profile: Edit your account information in Settings → Profile
  • Correct Errors: Request corrections to inaccurate data by contacting support

7.3 Deletion

  • Delete Account: Settings → Account → Delete Account (all data permanently deleted within 30 days)
  • Delete Individual Items: Delete specific meal logs, photos, or weight entries within the App

7.4 Consent Withdrawal

  • Notifications: Disable in Settings → Notifications or device settings
  • Camera/Microphone: Revoke permissions in device settings (app will still function with manual input)
  • Marketing Communications: Opt-out via unsubscribe link in emails

7.5 Do Not Track

Our App does not track users across third-party apps or websites. We do not respond to Do Not Track signals because we do not track users in the first place.

7.6 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable - we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mitara.ai
